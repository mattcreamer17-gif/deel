# For Deel

A bespoke, single-page landing built for Deel's R&D, platform, and AI Workforce leadership. The page is designed to feel like Deel's own marketing team produced it: deep indigo and purple gradients, sharp Inter type with tight tracking, stat-dense numbered blocks, and the declarative comma-chain sentence cadence that runs across deel.com. Content carries Eduba's 60/30/10 orchestration frame applied to the 150-country payroll engine and the seven AI Workforce agents, leads with the Correlation One reference at Pacific Life and Colgate-Palmolive (1,500+ trained, 6,000 to 9,000 hours saved, 95% 30-day adoption), cites the Ethics Engine paper as the agent-governance reference, and routes to a 30-minute conversation with Matt Creamer, CRO at Eduba. All URLs open in a new tab, pricing is stripped, and the only Eduba branding lives in the footer.
